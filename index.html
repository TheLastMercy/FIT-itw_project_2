Itachi Uchiha

A prodigy. A villain. A hero in the shadows.
Welcome to the story of a man who carried the weight of peace through sacrifice.

Biography

Childhood & Early Genius

Born during war, Itachi witnessed the cruelty of conflict from a young age. By age 7, he was outperforming academy seniors; by 13, he became the youngest ANBU captain in history.

Uchiha Clan Downfall

Ordered by Konoha elders to prevent a coup, Itachi made an impossible choice — sacrificing his clan to save the village. Only one survived: his younger brother, Sasuke.

Akatsuki & The Masked Life

As a double agent, he joined the rogue Akatsuki group. To the world, a criminal. In truth, a silent protector watching over Sasuke from afar.

Timeline of Key Events

  • Age 4: Witnesses Great Ninja War
  • Age 7: Graduates from the academy
  • Age 13: Becomes ANBU captain
  • Clan Night: The Uchiha Massacre
  • Final Act: Dies by Sasuke’s hand, truth revealed

Abilities & Techniques

Sharingan & Mangekyō

Genjutsu, foresight, and perception mastery

Itachi's dojutsu allowed him to anticipate enemy moves and cast illusions with near-instant speed.

Tsukuyomi

Psychological torture in seconds

His most feared genjutsu, trapping opponents in endless suffering within moments in real time. Itachi can control space, time everything.

Amaterasu

Black flames that never die

Summoned from his eyes, these flames could incinerate anything — even other fire-based jutsu.

Susanoo

Spectral guardian with divine weapons

Summoning a massive ethereal warrior armed with the Sword of Totsuka and Yata Mirror, he became nearly untouchable.
